#### SumateranRoar

![Alt text](/projects/sumateran-roar.jpeg "Optional title")


#### Description
This is an asymmetrical 4 vs 1 multiplayer game, where 4 humans trapped in the jungle must find a way out from being chased by a Sumatran tiger.

---

#### Game Story
Four people out on a nature trip get lost in the tropical forest of Sumatra. As night falls, they realize they're not alone in the forest, as a Sumatran tiger lurks behind the trees.

Their sole goal is to find a way out before the tiger devours them. To open the way, they must work with four other people to activate MACHINES hidden in various parts of the forest.

---

#### Role
Game Developer

---

#### Technology & Frameworks
Unity, Netcode, Relay & Lobby Service.

---

#### Features:
- Join Lobby
- Run fast (human and tiger)
- Interact with machines (human)
- Pounce on humans (tiger)
- Disappear immediately after being pounced on (human)
- Damage machines (tiger)
- Open the exit door (human)
