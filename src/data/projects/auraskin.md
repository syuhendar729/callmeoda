#### App Name: AuraSkin

#### Description
This is an iOS application to detect the ingredients of a skincare product whether it is good for our skin type or not. I as a programmer developed this application with my 4 teammates using SwiftUI, CoreData, and Cosmily API.

#### Role
iOS Developer

#### Technology & Frameworks
SwiftUI, CoreData, and Cosmily API.

#### Features:
- Detecting ingredient text from skincare products.
- Analyze the ingredients to see if they suit your skin type.
