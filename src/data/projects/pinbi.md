#### Title: PinBI

#### Description
This is a macOS app to help students correct their Indonesian writing. I am here as a programmer who creates the front-end view with SwiftUI, connections with Core Data, and creates a backend server with Nodejs for Prompting to GeminiAPI.

#### Role
MacOS Developer and Backend Developer

#### Technology & Frameworks
SwiftUI, SwiftData, GeminiAPI, Express, and Nodejs.

#### Features:
- Analysis of Indonesian writing according to EYD.
