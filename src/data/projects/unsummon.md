#### Unsummon: Super Scary Game

![Alt text](/projects/unsummon.jpeg "Optional title")


#### Description
This is a fun and horror themed multiplayer iOS game. I am here as a programmer who makes multiplayer connections with Unity, Netcode, and Relay service.

#### Role
Game Developer

#### Technology & Frameworks
Unity, Netcode, Relay & Lobby Service, Corehaptics, Gyroscope, and Speech Framework.

#### Features:
- Joining Lobby.
- Haptic if in the radius of the ghost.
- Gyroscope in hiden mechanic.
- Speech Recognition in uncurse candle.
- If become a Ghost can kill the children and curse candle.
- If become a Children can activate and uncruse candle.
- If it has been killed it can become a spirit to spectacle.
- Noise in spirit to deceive the ghost.
- Run or jump for increase speed.
- Realase Date
- November 2024
